Your Personalized Itinerary

Paris, France - 5 Days

Day 1

Eiffel Tower

Start your journey with a visit to the iconic Eiffel Tower. Enjoy panoramic views of Paris from the summit and explore the exhibits in the museum.

Day 2

Louvre Museum

Delve into the rich history of art and culture at the Louvre Museum. See masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Day 3

Seine River Cruise

Take a leisurely cruise along the Seine River and enjoy the beautiful architecture and landmarks of Paris from the water.

Day 4

Montmartre

Explore the charming neighborhood of Montmartre, known for its artistic heritage, charming streets, and the famous Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Day 5

Champs-Élysées

End your trip with a stroll along the famous Champs-Élysées, one of the most prestigious avenues in the world, and enjoy shopping and dining in the area.
